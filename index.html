1. Love – “In Quiet Glances” (Romantic, free verse)

In quiet glances, worlds collide, and silence speaks the loudest truths. Your laughter lingers like morning sun on windows never touched by rain. I never needed grand declarations — just the way your eyes softened when they looked at me.

2. Nature – “Whispers of the Forest” (Lyrical, rhymed)

The forest sings in ancient tune, with leaves that dance beneath the moon. A breeze that hums through moss and bark, ignites the soul, ignites the spark. Where shadows stretch and rivers gleam, the earth itself begins to dream.

3. Inner Strength – “Steel Beneath the Skin” (Modern, powerful)

I break and bend — but never fold. Each crack, a lesson. Each scar, a map. There is steel beneath the skin, and fire behind the calm. I rise. Always.

4. Time – “Hourglass” (Minimalist, reflective)

Grains slip — not lost, but lived. We chase tomorrow on the spine of today. Time never waits — but it always leaves a trace.
