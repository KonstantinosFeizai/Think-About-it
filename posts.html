Posts

Words that linger. Thoughts that grow.

arrow_drop_down

1. Love – “In Quiet Glances” (Romantic, free verse)

In quiet glances, worlds collide, and silence speaks the loudest truths. Your laughter lingers like morning sun on windows never touched by rain. I never needed grand declarations — just the way your eyes softened when they looked at me.

2. Nature – “Whispers of the Forest” (Lyrical, rhymed)

The forest sings in ancient tune, with leaves that dance beneath the moon. A breeze that hums through moss and bark, ignites the soul, ignites the spark. Where shadows stretch and rivers gleam, the earth itself begins to dream.

3. Inner Strength – “Steel Beneath the Skin” (Modern, powerful)

I break and bend — but never fold. Each crack, a lesson. Each scar, a map. There is steel beneath the skin, and fire behind the calm. I rise. Always.

4. Time – “Hourglass” (Minimalist, reflective)

Grains slip — not lost, but lived. We chase tomorrow on the spine of today. Time never waits — but it always leaves a trace.

5. Echoes

The silence holds a thousand cries, Unspoken truths and whispered lies. We smile and nod and play our parts, While hiding storms inside our hearts.

6. The Clock Doesn’t Wait

The clock ticks on, so unaware, Of dreams we dropped, of time unfair. But even broken hopes can mend— Start now, not later, not the end.

7. Between the Lines

Read me not by what I say, But by the things I push away. Between my words, a world you’ll find— Of tangled thoughts and untold mind.

8. Paper Wings

She folded dreams in paper wings, And sent them flying over things That tried to weigh her spirit down— But dreams, not chains, are what she found.

9. Hope – “Lightbreak” (Inspirational, free verse)

When the night feels endless, and the stars forget to shine, wait. Even the longest dark bows to the smallest light. And somewhere, a dawn is learning your name.

10. Loneliness – “Empty Chair” (Melancholic, rhymed)

The coffee cools, untouched, alone, Beside the chair where you've outgrown. The echo of your laugh remains, A soft refrain of distant rains. The room still speaks in silent ways — I listen, through the quiet days.

11. Joy – “Spilled Sunshine”

Laughter leaked from the seams of the day, spilled sunshine all over the gray.

12. Identity – “Mirror Talk”

The mirror says nothing — but asks everything. Who are you when no one’s watching? When you strip off the roles, the smiles, the noise — are you still whole? Or just practicing being real?
